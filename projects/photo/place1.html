Brazzaville

Brazzaville is the capital of Republic of Congo. It is located on the bank of the Congo River. The airport there is called Maya Maya Airport. Across the river is Kinshasa, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Congo. The taxis here are green which represent the jungle
