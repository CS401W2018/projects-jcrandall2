Interests Hero

Nikola Jokić

Nikola Jokić is the greatest player in the national basketball association and has been for the last 5 years. He is a force of nature who can pass, shoot, rebound, and defend. Averaging a triple double every night he plays is alien level. The only player who can compare to his triple double prowess is Russell Westbrook.

Russell Westbrook

Russell Westbrook is NOT washed. He is a veteran hooper who still has a ton to offer to a team such as the Denver Nuggets. He is a great passer can still get to the rim at will. He is a freak athlete and has had a triple double some of the same nights as Jokic getting one as well.

Jamal Murray

Jamal Murray aka Blue Arrow is the owner of the Los Angeles Lakers Every time the Lakers play Denver Jamal Murray cannot miss. I also really like playing in his signature shoe, the New Balance 2WXY V4.
