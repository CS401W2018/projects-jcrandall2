What are special entity characters?

Special entity characters are characters that cannot be directly typed into code. They have to be typed in a special way like the greater than or less than symbol.

What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags are things that modify text such as "Emphasis" or "Bold" to show different importances for different words or phrases.

What is the difference between OL and UL tags

The difference between OL and UL tags are OL puts Items in a sequential list and UL makes a list where order doesn't matter.
