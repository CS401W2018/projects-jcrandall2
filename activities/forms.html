What is JavaSript? How did it get its name?

JavaScript is a programming language. It was originally developed by Brendan Eich in 1995 under the name Mocha, later renamed to LiveScript, and finally to JavaScript. The name change was part of a marketing strategy to capitalize on the popularity of Java at the time.

What are the two ways you can run javascript?
