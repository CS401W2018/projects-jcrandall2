What's the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take up the entire width of the page, while inline tags only take up the width of their content.

What is margin, border and padding?

Margin is the space outside of an element, border is the line around an element, and padding is the space inside an element.

What are the units of measurement you can use for CSS?

There are many units of measurement you can use for CSS, including pixels (px), ems (em), rems (rem), percentages (%), and viewport units (vw, vh).

When would you use a figure tag?

You would use a figure tag when you want to group an image with its caption, such as a photo with a description.
