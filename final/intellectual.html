College

I love college at BYUH! I've learned so much coding and about Hawaii for my minors, and so much about the natural sciences such as biology and chemistry. Something I've come to accept is that learning is and always will be a part of my life. I'm never done learning things and I love it! I'm set to graduate in the spring, and I have some great future plans to further my academic career.

Dental School

I'm planning on applying to dental school in the fall of 2024. I have to retake Organic Chemistry II and Physics lab I, but I'm excited to get started on this journey of application and acceptance. I want to go to UNLV dental school because it's close to home and has a great program. I also want to be able to work with my dad and help him with his practice. Teeth and smiles are my future and I can't wait to become an expert in these fields of study and work.

More!

I want to learn how to sail as well so I can join my Dad on his sailing trips. So classes and certifications for sailing are on my list of things to do. I also want to learn how to scuba dive and get my certification. I want to be able to go on trips with my family and friends and explore the ocean.



Higher education creates opportunity and can set up my family for generations to come. An educated community performs better than their counterparts. Education is of God.
