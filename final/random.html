Luke 2:52 - "And Jesus increased in... Nunchuck skills, bowstaff skills..."

I've picked up quite a few hobbies while here in Hawaii that will bless me for the rest of my life. Here are a few of them:

Choir!

I did choir for the majority of my time at BYU Hawaii. I was in the Chamber Choir, which is the top choir on campus. I was able to perform at events in Honolulu and hold concerts on campus. Learning many songs in different languages was a great experience, and helped me connect with my Filipino, Japanese, and Polynesian peers on campus.

Video Games

I've perfected the art of playing Ness in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. I'm quite literally unstoppable with him. My children will be humbled and will know who's boss when I play with them.

Fixing Cars and Mopeds

If you're a broke college student like me, you'll learn how to fix your own car and moped. Learning these skills helped me to save money on repairs and help my friends with their cars and mopeds, which have had their fair share of problems. My wife and kids will never have to go to a mechanic because I can fix anything!

Benefits of random skills

Random skills can help in random situations, they're fun to learn, and you discover more about yourself as you learn them.
