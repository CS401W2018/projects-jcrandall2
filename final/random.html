Random Progress

Video Games

I've perfected the art of playing Ness in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. I'm quite literally unstoppable with him. My children will be humbled and will know who's boss when I play with them

Fixing Cars and Mopeds

My wife and kids will never have to go to a mechanic because I can fix anything!
