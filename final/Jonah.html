Project Proposal

I chose this topic because I like the new youth program for the church and never got to experience it. This website will help me check in with myself, track progress in various aspects of my life, and share it with my family.

Page Content

  • Home Page: Introduction to the site and purpose of the project.
  • Physical Progress: Tracking fitness goals, workout plans, and health improvements.
  • Spiritual Progress: Reflection on spiritual growth, church participation, and scripture study.
  • Intellectual Progress: Books read, new skills learned, and educational achievements.
  • Random Progress: Some other achievements that don't really mean anything in the grand scheme of things.
  • Social Progress: Tracking relationships, community service, and social activities (includes a table).

Form and Table Plan

Form Fields: Name, Email, Progress Category, Notes, and Submission Date.

Table Data: Date, Social Activity, People Involved, Lessons Learned.

Content and References

I will collect information from personal experiences, fitness trackers, church resources, and educational websites.
